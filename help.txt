Quagmire1421 command guide
------------------------------
General Commands:
!echo (message)
The echo command will have the bot repeat what the user sent before deleting the message
------------------------------
Voice Commands:
!song
Will play whatever song is currently chosen by the admin
!bubble
Plays the bubble shooter sound effect
!leave
The bot will leave the current voice channel it is in
!soundcloud
Plays a really good song from a ceratin soundcloud
!fix
This command will attempt to fix a broken voice bot, likely due to spam
------------------------------
Big Gay game:
The Big Gay is an illness that can only afflict one person in the discord at a time.
!bigGay
Displays the current host of the Big Gay
!giveGay (username, not nickname)
Give the Big Gay to another person if you yourself have the Big Gay at the time
